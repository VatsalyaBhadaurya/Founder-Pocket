Founder Pocket — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The core promise: Nothing you capture in Founder Pocket ever leaves your device. There are no servers, no analytics, no cloud sync, and no third-party data sharing — ever.

What data we collect

Founder Pocket collects nothing from you. All data — notes, voice transcripts, meeting summaries, documents, expenses, and AI-generated responses — is stored locally on your device and nowhere else.

Documents & sensitive files

Documents you store (Aadhaar, PAN, passport, resume, pitch decks) are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM via Android's Jetpack Security library. The encryption key is hardware-backed when your device supports it. Raw file bytes never appear in any database, log, or backup unless you explicitly enable Android's backup service.

AI features

The AI assistant (Gemma 3 1B) runs entirely on-device using MediaPipe. Your queries and captures are processed locally — they are never sent to any model API, cloud provider, or third-party service.

Location data

Location is only accessed when you explicitly tap "Attach location" while capturing a note or parking spot. It is stored with that capture on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Microphone / Speech

The RECORD_AUDIO permission is only used when you tap the Voice capture button. Transcription is performed by Android's on-device speech recogniser. No audio is recorded in the background.

Biometric data

Biometric authentication (fingerprint/face) uses Android's BiometricPrompt API. Biometric templates are managed entirely by the OS — Founder Pocket never reads, stores, or processes your biometric data directly.

Notifications

Reminder notifications are scheduled locally via WorkManager. No notification content is sent to any server.

Analytics & crash reporting

There are none. Founder Pocket has no analytics SDK, no crash reporting service, and no telemetry of any kind.

Third-party libraries

Libraries used (ONNX Runtime, MediaPipe, Jetpack Security, Hilt, Room, Coil) are open-source and operate entirely on-device. None of them phone home or collect data.

Data retention & deletion

Your data exists only on your device. To delete it, uninstall the app or clear app data in device settings. There is no account, no server-side copy, and nothing to request deletion of from us.

Children

Founder Pocket is designed for adult professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone — including children — because we collect no data at all.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy in a material way, the updated version will be bundled in the next app update.
